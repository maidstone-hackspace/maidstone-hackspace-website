Introduction

Hackspaces (also known as Makerspaces) are a shared space in which artists, designers, makers, hackers, programmers, tinkerers, professionals and hobbyists can work on their projects, share knowledge and collaborate.

Our space is kitted out with tools to facilitate working on your projects. You can contact via a mailing list, email, and Chat. If you're interested please join our mailing list and come say hey.

If you would like to visit us click here for a map, and check the mailing list for when we are at the space, we recommend contacting us to avoid disappointment.
